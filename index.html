Fun projects I've built

VTour -Interactive Google Street View Hyperlapse

Google Cardboard application, that utilizes Cloudlet, a mobile-edge computing infrastructure, to render realtime, immersive google street view panorama video. We support city mode, which users can stop at each intersection, and choose which way to go next, as well as highway mode, where users travel in almost 110 miles/h to even 220 miles/h in our application.

Emoj[IN] - Bring Emojis to your LinkedIn Profile

A hackathon project, that utilizes Google Chrome Extension to simulate putting emojis to user profile in home page, news feed and people search pages, to enable users show more information on their LinkedIn profile, such as whether they are looking for a job, what's their most proficient skills, etc. Users are able to set at most 3 emojis in their personal page.

Emoj[IN] was the top final 15 teams at LinkedIn's intern hackathon out of more than 60 teams.
